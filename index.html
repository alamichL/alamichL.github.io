I'm a post-doctoral researcher at Bocconi University in Milan working with Daniele Durante. I am part of the ERC project NEMESIS.

I did a PhD in Applied Mathematics at Centre Inria de l'Université Grenoble Alpes in Statify team, under the supervisation of Julyan Arbel and Guillaume Kon Kam King . During my PhD, I did a visiting research in the Department of Statistical Sciences at Duke University (USA) working with Filippo Ascolani. My thesis focuses on the inference of the number of clusters in Bayesian non-parametric mixture models, particularly on the asymptotic properties of these models. My thesis manuscript is available here.

Before, I did the Stat-ML Master at Université Paris-Saclay in 2021.
