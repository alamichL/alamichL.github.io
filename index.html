I'm a post-doctoral researcher at Bocconi University working with Daniele Durante. I am part of the ERC project NEMESIS.

I did a PhD in Applied Mathematics at Centre Inria de l'Université Grenoble Alpes in Statify team, under the supervisation of Julyan Arbel and Guillaume Kon Kam King . My thesis work focused on inference of the number of clusters in Bayesian non-parametric mixture models, and in particular on the asymptotic properties of these models.

Before, I did Stat-ML Master at Université Paris-Saclay in 2021.
